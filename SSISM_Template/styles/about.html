Why work with us?

Sant Singaji Educational Society (SSES) was established in 2010, with a view to create social and economic transformation in one of the most backward areas of the country, viz. Gondwana area of Madhya Pradesh. It runs a college – Sant Singaji Institute of Science and Management in Sandalpur village, Dewas District, Madhya Pradesh. This educational institute caters to the college going youth of three rural districts of Madhya Pradesh and educates around 1000 students annually.

MODERN EDUCATION

Rural youth have the capacity and urge to excel in studies. What they need is education in line with modern needs. We have good teachers and facilities to give students a modern education.

INDUSTRY LINKAGE

Today’s job market requires candidates who have the right set of skills and attitude. Rural students lack that, and therefore we send them for internships and trainings in companies and institutes like IITs, IIMs etc.

IMPACT

Students from SSISM are now placed in top companies like HSBC, SAP Labs, Wipro, Infosys, Cognizant, TCS, Flipkart. Students have also gone for higher studies in institutes like Azim Premji University, Banasthali Vidyapeeth etc. Many students are working in local industries, schools and colleges.

LIFELONG SUPPORT

We stay connected with our students even after they graduate, and help them move smoothly along their career paths.
